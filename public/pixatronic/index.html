Pixatronic

Pixatronic is an art tool for creating pixel art sprites quickly and easily. You draw 5-color sprites and block out shapes and patterns and let procedural generation do the rest. It picks out colors and shades for you, letting you focus more on your intent and less on the nitty-gritty details.

It’s a great art tool for the hobbyist or the gamejammer alike, encouraging rapid iteration and a more directed approach. Pixatronic is the blunderbuss to the more traditional pixel app’s scalpel, giving just enough control to make decisions and get output in the minimum amount of time.
